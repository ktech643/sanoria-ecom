Skincare Tips by Skin Type

Dry Skin Tips
  • Use cream-based cleansers
  • Apply moisturizer on damp skin
  • Look for hyaluronic acid
  • Avoid over-washing
Oily Skin Tips
  • Use gel-based cleansers
  • Don't skip moisturizer
  • Look for niacinamide
  • Use oil-free products
Combination Skin Tips
  • Use different products for different areas
  • Gentle cleansing is key
  • Multi-masking works well
  • Avoid harsh scrubbing
Sensitive Skin Tips
  • Patch test new products
  • Avoid fragrances
  • Use gentle, minimal ingredients
  • Apply sunscreen daily
