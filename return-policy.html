Return & Exchange Policy

1. Return Window

We offer a generous 14-day return window from the date you receive your order. This gives you ample time to try our products and ensure they meet your expectations.

Important Dates

  • Return Period: 14 calendar days from delivery date
  • Return Request: Must be initiated within the 14-day window
  • Item Shipment: Items must be shipped back within 7 days of return approval

2. Eligible Items for Return

The following items are eligible for return under our standard policy:

Eligible Items

  • Skincare products (unopened and unused)
  • Beauty tools and accessories
  • Items in original packaging with all tags/seals intact
  • Products that arrived damaged or defective
  • Wrong items sent by mistake

Non-Eligible Items

  • Opened or used skincare products (for hygiene reasons)
  • Items without original packaging
  • Custom or personalized products
  • Items purchased with promotional codes (unless defective)
  • Products returned after the 14-day window

3. Return Conditions

To ensure a smooth return process, please ensure your items meet the following conditions:

Product Condition Requirements

  • Unused State: Products must be in their original, unused condition
  • Original Packaging: All original packaging, including boxes, bottles, and protective materials
  • Tags and Seals: All tags, seals, and authenticity stickers must be intact
  • Accessories: Any included accessories, manuals, or free samples must be returned

Hygiene and Safety Standards

Important: For health and safety reasons, we cannot accept returns of opened skincare products, cosmetics that have been used, or any items that show signs of use or tampering.

4. How to Initiate a Return

Follow these simple steps to start your return process:

1
Contact Us

Email us at returns@sanoria.pk or call +92 300 1234567 with your order number and reason for return.

2
Get Return Authorization

We'll provide you with a Return Authorization Number (RAN) and return shipping instructions.

3
Package Your Items

Securely package your items in the original packaging and include the RAN in the package.

4
Ship the Return

Use our prepaid return label or ship via our recommended courier partners. We'll cover the return shipping costs.

5. Exchange Policy

We offer exchanges for the following situations:

Exchange Scenarios

  • Wrong Size/Shade: If you received the wrong size or shade of a product
  • Defective Items: Products that arrived damaged or defective
  • Wrong Item: If we sent you the wrong product by mistake

Exchange Process

Exchanges follow the same process as returns. Once we receive your returned item, we'll send you a replacement. If the replacement item has a different price:

  • If the new item costs more, you'll be charged the difference
  • If the new item costs less, we'll refund the difference

6. Refund Process

Our refund process is designed to be quick and transparent:

Refund Timeline

1-2 Days
Item Inspection

Once we receive your return, we'll inspect the item within 1-2 business days.

1 Day
Refund Approval

If your return meets our conditions, we'll approve the refund immediately.

5-7 Days
Payment Processing

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days back to your original payment method.

Refund Methods

  • Original Payment Method: Refunds go back to your original payment method
  • Store Credit: Option for faster processing as store credit
  • Bank Transfer: For cash on delivery orders, we'll need your bank details

7. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, we'll make it right immediately:

What We Cover

  • Items damaged during shipping
  • Manufacturing defects
  • Missing components or accessories
  • Products that don't match the description

Reporting Process

Step 1: Take photos of the damaged item and packaging

Step 2: Contact us within 48 hours of delivery

Step 3: We'll provide immediate replacement or full refund

Step 4: Keep the damaged item until we arrange pickup (if needed)

8. Return Shipping

We make returns convenient by covering return shipping costs:

Free Return Shipping

  • We provide prepaid return labels for most returns
  • Use our recommended courier partners: TCS, Leopard, or PkDex
  • Return shipping is free for defective or wrong items
  • Customer-initiated returns are also covered by us

Return Packaging Guidelines

  • Use the original packaging when possible
  • Secure items properly to prevent damage during transit
  • Include all original accessories and documentation
  • Write your Return Authorization Number clearly on the package

9. Special Circumstances

Late Returns

Returns initiated after the 14-day window may be considered on a case-by-case basis for:

  • Defective products discovered after initial use
  • Medical reactions to products (with medical documentation)
  • Exceptional circumstances beyond customer control

Bulk Orders

For orders over Rs. 10,000 or containing 10+ items, please contact our customer service team for specialized return assistance.

Gift Returns

Items purchased as gifts can be returned by the recipient. The refund will be issued as store credit unless the original purchaser requests otherwise.
