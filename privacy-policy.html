Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 15, 2024

1. Information We Collect

At Sanoria.pk, we collect information that you provide directly to us and information that is automatically collected when you use our services.

Personal Information You Provide

  • Name and contact information (email, phone number, address)
  • Account credentials (username, password)
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party providers)
  • Skin type and beauty preferences
  • Order history and preferences
  • Customer service communications
  • Reviews and ratings

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (IP address, browser type, operating system)
  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent, clicks)
  • Location information (general geographic location)
  • Cookies and tracking technologies data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Service Provision

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Provide customer support
  • Manage your account and preferences
  • Send order confirmations and shipping updates

Personalization

  • Recommend products based on your skin type
  • Customize your shopping experience
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Show relevant promotions and offers

Communication

  • Send promotional emails (with your consent)
  • Notify you about new products and features
  • Provide beauty tips and skincare advice
  • Respond to your inquiries and feedback

Business Operations

  • Analyze usage patterns and improve our services
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights and property

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

Service Providers

  • Payment processors (JazzCash, EasyPaisa, banks)
  • Shipping companies (TCS, Leopard, PkDex)
  • Technology service providers
  • Customer support platforms

Legal Requirements

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights and property
  • To ensure user safety and security
  • In case of business transfers or mergers

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Audits: Regular security assessments and updates
  • Secure Storage: Data stored in secure, access-controlled environments
  • Payment Security: We do not store payment card information

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access and Portability

  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • View your order history and account information

Correction and Updates

  • Update your account information
  • Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Modify your preferences and settings

Deletion and Opt-out

  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Opt-out of certain data processing activities

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services to you
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Improve our services and user experience

When we no longer need your information, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will delete that information promptly.

9. International Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside Pakistan. We ensure that such transfers are subject to appropriate safeguards to protect your personal information.

10. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Sending an email notification to registered users
  • Displaying a prominent notice on our platform

Your continued use of our services after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
