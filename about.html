Our Story

Founded with a passion for beauty and wellness

How It All Started

Sanoria.pk was born from a simple observation: Pakistani women deserved access to the same high-quality beauty products available worldwide. Founded in 2019, we started as a small online boutique with a big dream - to revolutionize the beauty industry in Pakistan.

Our founder, a beauty enthusiast herself, noticed the gap between the products available locally and the global standards of skincare and cosmetics. This sparked the idea to create a platform that would bring premium, authentic beauty products directly to Pakistani consumers.

Our Mission

To democratize access to premium beauty and skincare products in Pakistan, making quality cosmetics affordable and accessible to everyone. We believe that every person deserves to feel confident and beautiful in their own skin.

  • Provide authentic, high-quality products
  • Offer personalized beauty solutions
  • Educate customers about skincare
  • Support local beauty communities

Our Vision

To become Pakistan's most trusted and beloved beauty brand, known for our commitment to quality, authenticity, and customer satisfaction. We envision a future where every Pakistani has access to world-class beauty products.

  • Leading beauty destination in Pakistan
  • Setting industry standards for quality
  • Expanding to physical stores nationwide
  • Building a beauty-conscious community
